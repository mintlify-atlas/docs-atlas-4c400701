## What is a Lightning Address?

A Lightning address is a simple, email-like identifier that makes receiving Bitcoin as easy as sharing your email address. Instead of generating new invoices for each payment, you can share one permanent address.

<Note>
  Your Lightning address format: `username@wallet.medusa.bz`
  
  Example: If your username is "alice", your Lightning address is `alice@wallet.medusa.bz`
</Note>

## Benefits of Lightning Addresses

- **Easy to Remember**: Like an email address, much simpler than long invoice strings
- **Reusable**: One address for all incoming payments
- **Flexible Amounts**: Senders can specify any amount within your limits
- **Always Active**: No expiration like traditional invoices
- **Professional**: Clean, shareable format for business use

## Your Lightning Address

When you create a Medusa Wallet account, a Lightning address (called a "paylink") is automatically created for your default wallet.

### Address Details

- **Format**: `[username]@wallet.medusa.bz`
- **Linked to**: Your default wallet
- **Minimum**: 1 sat
- **Maximum**: 10,000,000 sats (10M sats)
- **Comments**: Supports messages up to 250 characters

## How to Share Your Lightning Address

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find Your Address">
    Navigate to the Receive screen. If you're using your default wallet without specifying an amount, your Lightning address will be displayed.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Copy or Share">
    Your Lightning address can be:
    - **Copied to clipboard**: Tap the copy button
    - **Shown as QR code**: Display the QR code for scanning
    - **Shared verbally**: It's short enough to read aloud
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add to Profiles">
    Use your Lightning address on:
    - Social media profiles
    - Email signatures
    - Business cards
    - Websites and blogs
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Receiving with Lightning Address

When someone pays your Lightning address:

1. **They enter your address** in their Lightning wallet
2. **They specify the amount** (within your min/max limits)
3. **They optionally add a comment** (up to 250 characters)
4. **Payment is instant** to your default wallet
5. **You receive notification** with amount and any comment

<Tip>
  All payments to your Lightning address go to your **default wallet**. If you want payments to go to a different wallet, receive them using a specific invoice for that wallet.
</Tip>

## Understanding Paylinks

Behind your Lightning address is a "paylink" - a Lightning LNURL pay endpoint that makes Lightning addresses work.

### Paylink Configuration

- **Description**: "Medusa default PayLink"
- **Min sendable**: 1 sat
- **Max sendable**: 10,000,000 sats
- **Comment chars**: 250
- **Always active**: No expiration

### Technical Details

Your Lightning address resolves to:
- An LNURL pay code
- Metadata including your identifier
- Dynamic invoice generation
- Webhook for payment notifications

<Note>
  The paylink is created automatically during account registration and is tied to your default wallet's admin key.
</Note>

To verify a payment was received:
1. Check your default wallet balance
2. Review transaction history
3. Look for the comment/description
4. Match the amount and timestamp

### Multiple Addresses

<Note>

## Troubleshooting

### Payments Not Arriving

- **Check Default Wallet**: Ensure you're viewing your default wallet
- **Amount Limits**: Verify payment is between 1 and 10M sats
- **Network Issues**: Sender may need to retry
- **Wrong Address**: Confirm sender used correct address

### Address Not Working

- **Typos**: Verify exact username spelling
- **Domain**: Must be `@wallet.medusa.bz`
- **Sender Wallet**: Some older wallets don't support Lightning addresses
- **Fallback**: Generate a traditional invoice instead

### Comments Not Showing

- **Check Transaction**: Comment appears in transaction details
- **Character Limit**: Comments over 250 chars may be truncated
- **Sender Support**: Verify sender's wallet supports comments
