## Creating a New Account

Medusa Wallet requires an account to securely manage your Lightning wallets and payments.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Medusa Wallet">
    Launch the Medusa Wallet application on your device.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Navigate to Sign Up">
    If this is your first time, you'll see the sign-up screen. If you already have an account, tap "Sign In" instead.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Your Details">
    Fill in the registration form:
    - **Username**: Choose a unique username (no spaces)
    - **Email**: Enter a valid email address
    - **Password**: Create a secure password

    <Tip>
      Choose a strong password that you don't use for other services. This protects your Bitcoin funds.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete Registration">
    Tap the "Sign Up" button to create your account. A default wallet will be automatically created for you.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  During registration, Medusa Wallet automatically creates:
  - A default Lightning wallet
  - A Lightning address (paylink) for receiving payments
</Note>

## Signing In to Your Account

If you already have a Medusa Wallet account:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Sign In">
    From the welcome screen, tap "Sign In".
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Your Credentials">
    - **Identifier**: Enter your username or email
    - **Password**: Enter your account password
  </Step>

  <Step title="Access Your Wallet">
    Tap "Sign In" to access your wallets and funds.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Advanced Configuration

### Changing the LNbits URL

Medusa Wallet is powered by LNbits. By default, it connects to `wallet.medusa.bz`, but you can configure it to use your own LNbits instance.

<Warning>
  Only change this setting if you're running your own LNbits server. Using an incorrect URL will prevent the wallet from functioning.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access URL Settings">
    On the sign-up or sign-in screen, look for "Change LNbits URL" at the bottom.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Custom URL">
    Enter your custom LNbits server URL in the format: `https://your-server.com`
  </Step>

  <Step title="Save and Continue">
    Save the settings and proceed with sign-up or sign-in.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Best Practices

<Tip>
  **Security Tips**:
  - Never share your password with anyone
  - Use a unique password for Medusa Wallet
  - Keep your email address secure and accessible
  - Consider using a password manager
</Tip>

## Troubleshooting

### Registration Fails

If registration fails:
- Check that your username is unique and doesn't contain spaces
- Ensure your email format is valid
- Verify your internet connection
- Try a different username if the error persists

### Cannot Sign In

If you can't sign in:
- Double-check your username/email and password
- Ensure you're using the correct LNbits URL
- Verify your internet connection
- Try resetting your password if available
